  Using the App
  Open the Weather App in your web browser.
  Enter a location in the search bar and click the "Search" button.
  The app will display the current weather information for the location you entered, including the temperature, weather condition, wind speed, and humidity.
  To switch between metric and imperial units, click the "°C/°F" button in the top right corner of the app.
  To refresh the data, click the "Refresh" button in the top left corner of the app.
